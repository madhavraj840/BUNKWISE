Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Flashlight App ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to be as simple and privacy-respecting as possible: it turns your device's flash on and off, and does nothing else.

1. Offline-Only App

Flashlight App operates entirely on your device. It does not connect to the internet, does not communicate with any server, and does not require an account of any kind.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or usage data from users.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Location information
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information
  • Usage analytics
  • Photos, camera images, or camera data

3. Permissions Used by the App

The App turns on your device's camera flash (torch) to provide light. On modern Android versions this does not require the device camera permission to be granted, and the App does not request the Camera permission, capture images, or record video.

The App requests no other device permissions — no internet access, no storage access, no location access, and no contacts access.

4. Local Storage

The App may store basic preferences (such as your last-used settings) locally on your device using standard Android app storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

5. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not sell, rent, share, trade, or disclose personal information to third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

7. Data Security

The App does not operate or rely on any server, so there is no remotely stored data to secure. Any local preferences remain on your device and are protected by your device's own security (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

8. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not retain any data because we do not collect any data. Any local preferences stored by the App can be removed at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's storage through Android settings.
  • Uninstalling the App.

Uninstalling the App removes all App-related data stored on your device.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults, because the App does not collect personal information from anyone.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and reflected by the updated "Last Updated" date.

You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
