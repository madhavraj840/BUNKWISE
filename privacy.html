Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains clearly what data BunkWise collects, why, and how it is used. Last updated: March 2026.

Data you provide

All attendance data you paste or upload, and all result PDFs you upload for SGPA calculation, are processed entirely inside your own browser. This data is never transmitted to any server operated by BunkWise. Once you close the tab, everything is gone.

Google Analytics

BunkWise uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects information such as your approximate location (country and city), the device and browser you are using, the pages you visit, how long you spend on the site, and how you arrived at the site (for example, from a search engine or a direct link).

This information is collected using cookies and similar tracking technologies placed by Google. The data is sent to and stored by Google on servers which may be located outside your country. Google may use this data in accordance with its own privacy policy, which you can read at policies.google.com/privacy.

We use this data only to improve BunkWise — for example, to understand which features are most used and which devices we should optimise for. We do not sell this data or use it to identify you personally.

Cookies

BunkWise itself does not set any cookies. However, Google Analytics sets its own cookies (such as _ga and _gid) to distinguish visitors and track sessions. These cookies are governed by Google's cookie policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

AI-powered result analysis (SGPA Calculator)

When you use the Autonomous SGPA Calculator feature (for SKIT result PDFs), the raw text extracted from your uploaded PDF is sent to Google's Gemini AI API for analysis. This text contains your result data including your name, roll number, father's name, and subject marks as they appear in the PDF.

This data is transmitted to Google's servers to perform the analysis and is governed by Google's AI API Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. BunkWise does not store or log this data. The API call is routed through a Cloudflare Worker proxy — the Gemini API key is stored securely in Cloudflare and is never exposed in the website's code.

A rate limit of 2 analyses per device per day is enforced locally in your browser using localStorage to prevent excessive API usage. This counter is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

If you are not comfortable with your result data being processed by Google's AI, please use the RTU SGPA Calculator instead, which processes everything locally in your browser with no external API calls.

Third-party services

BunkWise loads the following external resources. Each is governed by its own privacy policy.

  • Google Analytics — visitor analytics. See above.
  • Google Gemini AI API — used for autonomous result PDF analysis. Result text is sent to Google's servers for processing. See the AI section above.
  • Google Fonts — typography. Google may log the font request; no personal data beyond a standard web request is shared.
  • Cloudflare CDN — delivers PDF.js, SheetJS, and jsPDF libraries. Cloudflare may log IP addresses per their standard CDN policy.
  • Cloudflare Workers — acts as a secure proxy for Gemini API calls. Cloudflare processes the request but does not store your result data.

Your rights

You have the right to opt out of analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on linked above, or by using your browser's private/incognito mode. If you have questions about how your data is handled, you can contact the site creator via the GitHub profile linked on the Creator Description page.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of BunkWise after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
