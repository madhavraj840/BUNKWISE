Privacy Policy

Your privacy is taken seriously. Here is exactly what happens with your data when you use BunkWise.

Zero data collection

BunkWise collects absolutely nothing. There are no databases, no analytics, no user tracking of any kind.

Local processing only

The attendance text you paste never leaves your device. All parsing and calculations run inside your own browser tab.

Cookies & trackers

BunkWise uses no cookies, no local storage, and no third-party tracking scripts.

Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties. The only external resource loaded is Google Fonts for typography.
