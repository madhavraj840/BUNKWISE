2. Description of Service

BunkWise provides three core tools accessible through this website:

  • Attendance Calculator — Parses ERP attendance data (pasted or uploaded as CSV/Excel) to show subject-wise and aggregate attendance percentages along with bunk/attendance counts.
  • SGPA Calculator — Reads RTU result PDFs or SKIT autonomous result PDFs to compute Semester Grade Point Averages with subject-wise breakdowns.
  • Class Wise Calculator — Projects future attendance percentages based on classes a student plans to attend or skip.

All tools are provided free of charge. BunkWise reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any part of the service at any time without prior notice.
