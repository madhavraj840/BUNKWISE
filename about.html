About Us

BunkWise is a fast, no-login attendance calculator built for college students who are tired of manually computing whether they can skip that Friday lecture.

What we do

Copy your attendance table from your college ERP portal, paste it into BunkWise, and get a full breakdown instantly — aggregate percentage, subject-wise percentages, and exactly how many classes you can bunk or must attend to hit your desired target.

How it works

All processing happens directly in your browser. No data is sent anywhere. No account needed. Close the tab and everything is gone.

Why we built it

So you can BUNK classes without any guilt!
