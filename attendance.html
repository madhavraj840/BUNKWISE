Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum attendance required at RTU colleges?

Rajasthan Technical University (RTU) and most of its affiliated colleges require a minimum of 75% attendance in each subject to be eligible for semester examinations. Some colleges may have a higher threshold (80%), so always check your institution's specific rules. BunkWise uses 75% as the default target, but you can adjust it using the ✎ button.

How does the BunkWise attendance calculator work?

Copy your attendance table from your college ERP portal (select all → Ctrl+C) and click "Paste Attendance" on BunkWise. The tool parses your data entirely in your browser, calculates your aggregate and subject-wise percentages, and instantly tells you how many classes you can bunk or must attend for each subject.

Can I use BunkWise if my college is affiliated to RTU?

Yes. BunkWise works for students at any RTU-affiliated college that uses a standard ERP portal for attendance — including Poornima University, VGU, Arya College, JECRC, SKIT, Manipal Jaipur, and hundreds of other institutions. If your ERP portal shows an attendance table, BunkWise can read it.

Is my attendance data safe?

Completely. BunkWise processes everything in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to any server, and nothing is stored or logged. When you close or refresh the tab, all data is gone. There is no login, no account, and no tracking of your attendance.

Does BunkWise support file upload as well as paste?

Yes. In addition to the clipboard paste method, BunkWise supports direct upload of CSV and Excel (.xlsx, .xls) files exported from your ERP portal. This is useful if your college's ERP portal doesn't allow easy copying of the attendance table.

What is the difference between aggregate attendance and subject-wise attendance?

Aggregate attendance is your overall percentage across all subjects. Subject-wise attendance is the individual percentage for each course. Most colleges check both — you need to maintain the minimum percentage in every individual subject, not just the aggregate. BunkWise shows you both so you're never caught off-guard.
