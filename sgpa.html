Frequently Asked Questions

What is SGPA and how is it calculated?

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is a weighted average of the grade points earned in a semester. Each subject's grade point is multiplied by its credit hours, and the sum is divided by the total credits. BunkWise reads your result PDF and performs this calculation automatically, showing you the final SGPA along with the full subject-wise breakdown.

Which result PDF formats does BunkWise support?

BunkWise supports two formats: (1) RTU standard PDFs downloaded from rtu.ac.in — use the RTU upload zone. (2) SKIT autonomous result PDFs from Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology Jaipur — use the Autonomous upload zone. More autonomous college formats will be added over time.

What is the difference between the RTU and Autonomous pipeline?

RTU-affiliated colleges use a standardised result format from rtu.ac.in. Autonomous colleges like SKIT have their own result PDF format with different columns (ISE/SEE instead of IA/EA), different grading schemes, and per-subject Pass/Fail remarks. BunkWise has separate parsers for each format to ensure accurate calculation for both.

Is my result PDF uploaded to any server?

No. Your PDF is read entirely in your browser using PDF.js. It is never uploaded to any server, never stored, and never shared. When you close the tab, everything is gone. BunkWise has no backend — it is a 100% client-side tool.

Can students from Poornima, VGU, Arya, and other colleges use this?

Yes, if your college is affiliated to RTU and your result PDF is downloaded from rtu.ac.in, BunkWise's RTU calculator will work for you — regardless of whether you're at Poornima, VGU, Arya, JECRC, or any other affiliated college. The SKIT autonomous calculator is specifically for SKIT Jaipur students at this time.

How do I get my RTU result PDF?

Visit rtu.ac.in and navigate to the Results section. Search for your result using your Roll Number. When the result opens in your browser, press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) and choose "Save as PDF" as the destination. Upload that saved PDF file to BunkWise.
