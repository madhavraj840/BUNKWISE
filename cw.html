Class Wise Attendance Calculator — Plan Your Future Attendance

The BunkWise Class Wise Calculator is the smartest attendance analysis tool for planning your future attendance at any RTU-affiliated college. It answers the key question every student asks: "What will my attendance be if I attend — or skip — a certain number of future classes?"

Most colleges under Rajasthan Technical University, including Poornima University, VGU (Vivekananda Global University), Arya College of Engineering, JECRC University, and SKIT Jaipur, enforce a strict 75% attendance requirement. Falling below this threshold can bar you from sitting for semester examinations. The Class Wise Calculator helps you plan ahead before it's too late.

Just paste your current attendance data from the ERP portal, then use the adjustor to add or subtract future classes. The college attendance calculator instantly recalculates your projected aggregate and tells you whether you're on track to meet your target.

This tool is especially useful in the weeks before exams when you want to know exactly how many more classes you absolutely need — or how many you can safely skip without jeopardising your 75 attendance calculator target. No more guessing or using Excel sheets.

Students at Rajasthan Technical University colleges use the Class Wise Calculator alongside the main attendance calculator to make informed decisions about their attendance strategy for the rest of the semester. It's the bunk calculator college feature taken to the next level.

The tool works with the same ERP paste or CSV/Excel upload method as the main attendance page — no extra setup needed. Your data stays entirely in your browser, just like every other BunkWise tool. No login, no server, completely private.

Whether you're a first-year student still figuring out college rules, or a final-year student managing a packed schedule, the Class Wise Calculator gives you the clarity you need to make the smartest possible attendance decisions for your situation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Class Wise Calculator different from the Attendance Calculator?

The main Attendance Calculator shows your current attendance status based on your existing data. The Class Wise Calculator goes a step further — it lets you simulate what your attendance will be after you attend or skip a certain number of future classes. It's a planning tool for the remainder of the semester.

What does "1 class = 1 hour" mean?

In RTU and its affiliated colleges, attendance is typically tracked in hours (1-hour slots). So when you adjust the class count using the ✎ button, each unit corresponds to 1 hour of class attendance. This matches the standard ERP attendance report format.

Can I calculate what happens if I attend all remaining classes?

Yes. Set the class adjustor to the number of remaining classes in the semester and see your projected attendance. You can also set it to a negative number to see what happens if you skip classes. The future attendance percentage updates live as you adjust.

Does the Class Wise tool work with CSV/Excel upload?

Yes. Just like the main Attendance Calculator, the Class Wise Calculator also supports uploading your attendance data as a CSV or Excel file exported from your ERP portal, in addition to the standard clipboard paste method.

Which colleges can use this tool?

Any student at an RTU-affiliated college whose ERP portal generates a standard attendance report can use this tool — including colleges like Poornima, VGU, Arya, JECRC, SKIT, Manipal Jaipur, JEC, and all other institutions affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University.
