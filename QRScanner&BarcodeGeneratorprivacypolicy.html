Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how QR Scanner & Barcode Generator ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data remains under your control. The App is designed with a privacy-first and offline-first approach.

1. Offline-First Privacy Approach

QR Scanner & Barcode Generator is designed to operate primarily on your device. QR code scanning, barcode scanning, QR code generation, scan history management, and related features are processed locally whenever possible.

We do not operate servers that collect, store, process, sell, rent, or share your scan history, generated QR code content, or personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from users.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Location information
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information
  • Usage analytics
  • Scan history
  • Generated QR code content

3. Scan History

When you scan a QR code or barcode, the scan result may be stored locally on your device to provide scan history functionality.

This information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us. You may delete individual entries or clear your history at any time.

4. QR Code and Barcode Generation

Any text, URLs, contact information, Wi-Fi credentials, phone numbers, email addresses, or other content entered for QR code generation remain on your device.

We do not access, collect, transmit, or store this information.

5. Local Storage

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Scan history
  • Generated QR codes
  • Exported files
  • User preferences and settings

All such information remains under your control and is not transmitted to us.

6. Permissions Used by the App

The App requests only the permissions required to provide its functionality.

  • Camera Permission
    Used solely to scan QR codes and barcodes using your device camera.
  • Photo Picker / Gallery Access
    Used to allow you to scan QR codes and barcodes from images that you explicitly select. The App accesses only the images you choose.
  • Wi-Fi Access
    Used to assist with Wi-Fi QR code functionality and allow convenient connection to Wi-Fi networks encoded in QR codes.
  • Vibration
    Used to provide haptic feedback after successful scans.
  • Internet Access
    Used when you choose to open links, websites, or online resources contained within scanned QR codes.

7. Exported Files

If you choose to export scan history or generated QR code information (such as CSV or TXT files), those files are created and stored locally on your device or in a location selected by you.

We do not receive, access, or store exported files.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google ML Kit for QR code and barcode recognition.

Barcode scanning is performed on-device using Google's machine learning technology. Scan content is not transmitted to us.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

9. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not sell, rent, share, trade, or disclose personal information to third parties.

10. Privacy Mode

The App may provide a Privacy Mode feature. When enabled, the App uses Android system security mechanisms to help prevent its contents from appearing in the Recent Apps screen.

This feature is intended to help reduce accidental disclosure of scanned information.

11. Data Security

The App does not maintain servers that store user scan data, generated QR code content, or personal information.

Information stored by the App remains on your device. Users are responsible for securing access to their devices and any locally stored information.

12. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not retain personal information because we do not collect personal information.

Data stored locally on your device remains under your control.

You may delete data by:

  • Deleting scan history within the App.
  • Removing exported files from your device.
  • Clearing the App's storage through Android settings.
  • Uninstalling the App.

Uninstalling the App generally removes all App-managed local data stored by the App.

13. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults because the App does not collect personal information from users.

14. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external websites or online services.

We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of third-party websites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any websites they visit.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and reflected by the updated "Last Updated" date.

You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
