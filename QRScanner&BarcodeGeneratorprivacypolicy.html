Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 08, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QR Scanner & Barcode Generator ("the App", "We", "Us", or "Our") handles information when you use the App.

The App is designed with a privacy-first approach and operates primarily on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, rent, or share your personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location information
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Contact information
  • Payment information

Camera Permission

The App requires camera permission solely for scanning QR codes and barcodes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

QR Code and Barcode Processing

All QR code and barcode scanning is performed locally on your device. The contents of scanned codes are not transmitted to our servers because we do not operate servers that receive scan data.

QR Code Generation

Any text, URLs, contact information, Wi-Fi credentials, or other content entered for QR code generation remains on your device. We do not access, collect, or transmit this information.

Local Storage

The App may store scan history, generated QR codes, exported files, and user preferences locally on your device to provide functionality requested by you.

This information remains under your control and is not transmitted to us. You may delete this data at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google ML Kit for barcode and QR code scanning functionality. Scanning is performed on-device and does not require us to collect your personal information.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not sell, rent, share, or disclose personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We do not retain personal information because we do not collect personal information.

Any scan history, generated QR codes, exported files, or application settings are stored locally on your device and remain under your control.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children because the App does not collect personal information from any users.

Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal information to our servers, risks associated with server-side data storage are minimized.

Users remain responsible for securing access to their own devices and any information stored locally on those devices.

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of third-party websites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any websites they visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page and reflected by the "Last Updated" date shown above.
