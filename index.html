About Us

BunkWise is a fast, no-login attendance calculator built for college students who are tired of manually computing whether they can skip that Friday lecture.

What we do

Copy your attendance table from your college ERP portal, paste it into BunkWise, and get a full breakdown instantly — aggregate percentage, subject-wise percentages, and exactly how many classes you can bunk or must attend to hit your desired target.

How it works

All processing happens directly in your browser. No data is sent anywhere. No account needed. Close the tab and everything is gone.

Why we built it

So you can BUNK classes without any guilt!

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is taken seriously. Here is exactly what happens with your data when you use BunkWise.

Zero data collection

BunkWise collects absolutely nothing. There are no databases, no analytics, no user tracking of any kind.

Local processing only

The attendance text you paste never leaves your device. All parsing and calculations run inside your own browser tab.

Cookies & trackers

BunkWise uses no cookies, no local storage, and no third-party tracking scripts.

Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties. The only external resource loaded is Google Fonts for typography.
